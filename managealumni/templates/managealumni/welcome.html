What is IESA?

IESA was formed in 2001 on the Gainesville campus of UNG. IESA promotes environmental education using advanced technology,
interdisciplinary instruction, collaborative learning, and service. The Institute brings together numerous disciplines through
the synthesis of Geospatial Technology, applied techniques, and theory to address social, human and environmental issues. Students, faculty, and staff
work together with industry, government, and community partners through their coursework resulting in a dynamic and socially relevant learning environment.
If you are interested in learning more about IESA and it's programs please click on the links at the bottom of the page.

What is the IIAA?

The IIAA is an informal alumni association being created for the purposes of networking and giving back to the institute that has given us all so much.
